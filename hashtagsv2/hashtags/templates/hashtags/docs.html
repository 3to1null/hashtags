What is a hashtag?

Downloading results

You can download CSV results for a hashtag at http://tools.wmflabs.org/hashtags/csv/<tag>?limit=<limit>. If you do not provide a limit parameter, it will return 20,000 revisions by default. You can also optionally provide a lang parameter to limit your results to one version of Wikipedia (or lang=wikidata), and startdate and/or enddate parameters to limit your search by date (date format YYYY-MM-DD).
